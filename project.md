# RoomPeace Web App Specification

## Project Overview
RoomPeace is a gamified co-living relationship platform designed for college students living in dormitories or shared housing.

Instead of managing chores like a productivity app, RoomPeace helps users prevent conflicts through game mechanics, emotional communication, and shared team goals.

Brand Message:
"Don't fight with your roommates. Solve it like a pirate crew."

---

## Target Users
College students (20–26 years old) living with roommates.

Main Pain Points:
1. Unclear chore responsibilities
2. Unequal contribution
3. Emotional discomfort in direct communication

---

## Design Concept

Theme:
Pirate crew / adventure team

Style:
- Warm and playful
- Mobile-first
- Rounded UI
- Friendly game interface

Color Palette:
- Navy
- Gold
- Cream

---

## Core Features

### 1. House Rule
Users create shared living rules.

Examples:
- Wash dishes within 24 hours
- Take out trash when full
- Bathroom cleaning once a week

---

### 2. Flash Mission
Real-time chore quests.

Examples:
- Take out trash (+30P)
- Wash dishes (+10P)

---

### 3. Peace Card
Emotional communication cards.

Cards:
- I'm exhausted this week
- Can we switch roles?
- Thanks for helping

---

### 4. Character Growth

Levels:
Crew → Officer → Captain

Users gain XP by completing missions.

Titles:
- Dish King
- Peace Keeper
- Clean Master

---

## MVP Scope

Version 1 should include:

- Onboarding
- House Dashboard
- House Rule Page
- Peace Card Page
- Character Growth Page
